Our Story

Three generations of baking tradition

What started in a small home kitchen in 1968 has grown into the neighbourhood's most beloved bakery. Every loaf, cake, and pastry is still made by hand using recipes passed down from our grandmother — with a few modern flourishes.

We source our flour from local mills, our butter from family-run dairies, and our seasonal fruits directly from nearby farms. Because the best ingredients make the best memories.
